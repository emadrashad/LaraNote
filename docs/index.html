Your Digital Highlighter & Notebook.

LaraNote is a browser extension that allows you to highlight text and take notes on any webpage. Your highlights and notes are saved locally, specific to the URL you are on.

Add to Chrome - It's Free

Everything You Need to Stay Organized

Capture information effortlessly and keep your notes organized by page.

Highlighting

Select any text on a webpage to bring up a small toolbar. Click the highlight button to highlight the selected text.

Notes

Add notes to your highlights. Keep your thoughts and ideas connected to the source material.

Copying

Easily copy the selected text to your clipboard with a single click from the toolbar.

Removing Highlights

Remove highlights with a single click, keeping your workspace clean and organized.

Per-URL Savings

All your highlights and notes are saved against the URL of the page you are on, keeping everything in context.

Export/Import

Export your notes as a JSON file for backup or to use in other applications. You can also import notes.

Simple and Intuitive

Get started in seconds. No account needed.

1

Select Text

Simply select any text on a webpage with your mouse.

2

Use the Toolbar

A small toolbar will appear. You can highlight, add a note, copy the text, or remove a highlight.

3

View & Manage Notes

Click on the extension icon to view, edit, or delete your notes for the current page.

Privacy-Focused

We only request the permissions we need to function. Your data is stored locally on your computer.

  • Storage

    To save your highlights and notes on your local device. LaraNote uses content scripts that run automatically, so no additional permissions are needed.
