Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 4, 2025

LaraNote is a lightweight browser extension designed for highlighting text and adding notes directly on webpages. This policy outlines how LaraNote handles your data. Your privacy is our highest priority.

1. Data Collection and Transmission

LaraNote DOES NOT collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, browsing history, or highlight information to any external server, database, or third party.

The extension operates entirely within your local browser environment.

2. Data Storage (Local Only)

All data created by you is saved only on your local machine using the browser's native storage APIs (specifically chrome.storage.local).

  • Data Stored: Text highlights, corresponding notes, and the location needed to restore them.
  • Data Security: This data is stored securely in your Chrome profile and is not accessible to us or any other party.
  • Data Control: Deleting the extension or clearing your browser data will permanently delete your saved highlights.

3. Permissions Explained

LaraNote requires only one permission to perform its core function:

"storage"

This permission allows the extension to save and retrieve your notes and highlights from your browser's local storage. This ensures your data persists when you close and reopen the browser. LaraNote uses content scripts that run automatically on web pages, so no additional permissions are needed to display the highlighting toolbar.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the Effective Date. Since no external data is collected, any changes will only affect how the extension functions locally.
